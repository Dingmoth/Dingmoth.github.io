What Is Paid Social?

Paid social media is a way to display adverts or other sponsored content via social media platforms. These apps usually have in-app tools to create, schedule and post your ads. These ads can be very useful for targeting specific audiences, based on core demographics like age, location and even interests.

  • Retargeting: Maybe your company already has a significant social media presence from your organic strategy, but you’re looking to remain engaged with previous or current customers? Paid social ads can help you re-engage with existing customers or followers and promote your latest developments.
  • Increase brand awareness: Paid social media advertising is a great way to boost your brand awareness, especially for SMEs. If your content appears in users’ newsfeeds regularly, brand recognition will naturally increase.
  • Cost-effective: All platforms offer a pay-per-click model, meaning you pay only when users engage with your content.
  • Ability to measure success: For marketers, it’s crucial to know how well your ad campaigns are doing. With paid social adverts, it’s easy to measure the performance of your ads – whether you are looking at clicks, reach or engagement. You’ll be able to easily measure ROI and measure the overall success of your ad campaigns.
  • Variety of formats available for ads: With many different platforms to choose from and different types of adverts, paid social media advertising will allow you to cater to different audiences and preferences.
  • Increase reach and target audiences more effectively: Most social media platforms enable users to use interest-based targeting to ensure ads are reaching the people who are truly interested in your content. You’ll also be able to reach customers that might not know your brand but are interested in your product.
