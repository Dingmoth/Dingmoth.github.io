What are the Benefits of SEO

You might be wondering: ‘ what even is SEO and what does it have to do with me?’ Keep reading! SEO is the process of getting your website ranked highly in the Search Engine Results page. Having a well thought-out SEO strategy is one of the most important and profitable things you can do for your business

  • Customer Satisfaction: Part of SEO is getting people to interact with your page longer, and offering them quality content to engage with. This increases your customer satisfaction and will boost your SEO.
  • Quality Traffic: Users coming from organic search on average spend twice as long on your website as those coming from PPC. There is a higher level of trust in organic results as users realise what the site has done to earn their spot.
  • Local Searches: 78% of searches for local businesses result in a purchase. SEO utilises techniques to help ranking your website in the map pack for local searches. SEO utilises smart techniques to help you appear for local searches.
  • Free Traffic: An effective SEO strategy will improve the flow of free or ‘organic’ traffic coming from Google through to your website.
  • Increased Visibility: SEO allows you to choose what keyword you want to appear on Google for, and with the implementation of a strategy will see your website “rank” for these keywords on the first page.
  • Save Your Cash! SEO does not involve an auction where you bid for a position like in Google Ads, but rather you can achieve this through time and hard work.
