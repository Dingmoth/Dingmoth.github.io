Let Us Manage Your Google Search Console

Controlling your webmaster tools is vital for initial website set up, as well as monitoring ongoing SEO. The Google Search Console should be set up for every website by placing a snippet of code provided by Google into the header of your website. This allows you to see if there are any manual actions against your domain, upload your sitemap, monitor crawl errors and website speed errors and much more.

Search Console also provides data similar to Google Analytics around your website’s traffic. You can check what keywords users are searching in order to find your business, or if there are any arising 404s on the website that need to be addressed. Evolution Digital will help you set up your search console, and our SEO experts have the experience needed to manage it on an ongoing basis. Get in touch with us today.
